### Pack 132: Touching & Playing Music (tocar) - Score 4/10
**Severity:** CRITICAL  
**Issue:** Systematic translation error affecting all rows  
**Problem:** Spanish verb "tocar" (to play music/instrument) incorrectly translated as Chinese "玩" (to play games/toys) instead of "演奏"/"弹奏" (to play music/perform)  
**Impact:** 10 rows affected - entire pack has wrong meaning  
**Example:**  
- Spanish: "tocar la guitarra" (play the guitar)
- Current Chinese: Uses "玩" (play with - as in toys)
- Should be: "演奏吉他" or "弹吉他" (play/perform guitar)
