HIGH PRIORITY FIXES - 一 (YĪ) TONE SANDHI ERRORS
================================================

RULE REMINDER:
- 一 + 4th tone → yí (2nd tone)
- 一 + 1st/2nd/3rd tone → yì (4th tone)
- 一 alone or counting → yī (1st tone)
