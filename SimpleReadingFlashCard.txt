DATA SOURCES:
- Module: https://some-dude-999.github.io/LPH/SpanishWords/Jsmodules/act1-foundation.js
  Provides wordpacks with Spanish vocabulary and translations (english, chinese, portuguese)
- LocalStorage: Saves user preferences (voice selection, speech speed)
- Web Speech API: Text-to-speech for pronunciation

FUNCTIONALITY:
- Main Purpose: Traditional flashcard practice - read Spanish, see English translation
- Auto-Start: Game automatically starts with Pack 1 and English language on page load
- Game Flow:
  1. Spanish word appears on front of card
  2. Click card or press Down arrow to flip
  3. English translation appears on back
  4. Mark card as "Got it right" (üëå) or "Need practice" (üòï)
  5. Navigate with left/right arrows
  6. Remove mastered cards from deck

- Key Features:
  * Spanish word on FRONT
  * English translation on BACK
  * Got it button (üëå) - Removes card from deck
  * Need practice button (üòï) - Adds 2 duplicate cards
  * Pronunciation button (üó£Ô∏è) - Hear Spanish pronunciation
  * Navigation arrows (‚Äπ ‚Ä∫) - Move between cards
  * Card counter - Shows progress (Card X of Y)

- Key Functions:
  * loadWordpacks() - Dynamically imports vocabulary modules and auto-starts game
  * initializeDeck(packKey) - Creates shuffled deck from selected wordpack
  * updateDisplay() - Renders Spanish word on front, English on back
  * removeCurrentCard() - Removes mastered card from deck (green stamp)
  * addConfusedCards() - Adds 2 copies of difficult card (red stamp)
  * speakSpanish() - Text-to-speech pronunciation
  * goToNext() / goToPrevious() - Navigate between cards
