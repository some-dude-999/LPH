Pack 4: Articles & Determiners (Score: 5/10)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  • Row 2: CRITICAL - Spanish 'a' is WRONG. 'a' means 'to' (preposition), not indefinite article. Should be 'un/una'

Pack 12: Family (Score: 7/10)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  • Row 14: Chinese '表哥' is too specific (older male maternal cousin) for neutral 'cousin'. Better: '表亲' (neutral cousin)

Pack 17: Modal Verbs (Score: 6/10)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  • Row 4: CRITICAL - Spanish 'voluntad' is WRONG. 'voluntad' = 'willpower/desire', not future modal. Modal 'will' in Spanish is conjugation (iré, irá, etc.), could use 'futuro'
  • Row 11: Spanish 'atrevimiento' is noun (boldness), but 'dare' is verb. Should be 'atreverse' or 'osar'

Pack 19: Essential Verbs: Go & Come (Score: 6/10)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  • Row 5: Chinese '消失了' (disappeared) not quite right for 'gone'. Better: '走了' or '离开了'
  • Row 5: Portuguese 'perdido' (lost) wrong for 'gone'. Should be 'ido' or 'foi embora'
  • Row 9: CRITICAL - Chinese '未来' means 'future' (noun), NOT 'coming'. Should be '来' or '正在来'
