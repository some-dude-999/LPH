ONE Spanish entry per row. This entry can be:

A single word: "gato"

NEVER combine alternatives with slashes. Each variant gets its own row.

PARENTHESIS MARKERS - LIMITED TO 5 OPTIONS
You may ONLY use these exact markers inside parentheses:

(masculine)      - for male-gendered nouns
(feminine)       - for female-gendered nouns
(formal)         - for formal "you" forms
(plural)         - when English translation is ambiguous
(singular)       - when English translation is ambiguous

NO OTHER MARKERS. No "he/she", no "NOT", no grammar terms.
================================================================================
TRANSLATION QUALITY STANDARDS - CRITICAL
NATURALNESS OVER LITERALNESS
All translations must sound natural and idiomatic in the target language.
Avoid word-for-word translations that sound awkward.
CHINESE TRANSLATION RULES:

Use natural, idiomatic Chinese expressions
Add appropriate particles (了, 一下, 吗, 呢) for completeness
Use context-appropriate vocabulary levels
Prefer common spoken forms over literary forms

COMMON CHINESE PATTERNS:

"please wait" → 请等一下 (NOT 请等 or 请等等)
"excuse me I'm passing" → 劳驾借过 (NOT 劳驾我过)
"sorry for that" → 对不起那件事 (NOT 对不起那个)
"excuse the bother" → 对不起打扰了 (NOT 对不起打扰)
"please help me" → 请帮助我 (NOT 请帮我 - too abrupt)

PORTUGUESE TRANSLATION RULES:

Use Brazilian Portuguese as the standard
Match formality level with Spanish original
Use idiomatic expressions, not literal translations
Ensure gender agreement matches Spanish where applicable

PINYIN RULES:

Must exactly match the Chinese characters provided
Include all tone marks (ā á ǎ à)
Use proper spacing for multi-syllable words
Include parenthetical markers in pinyin if in Chinese

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID
CHINESE:
WRONG: 请帮我 (too abrupt)
RIGHT: 请帮助我 (polite and complete)
WRONG: 请等等 (too casual/repetitive)
RIGHT: 请等一下 (natural and polite)
WRONG: 劳驾我过 (grammatically awkward)
RIGHT: 劳驾借过 (idiomatic expression)
WRONG: 对不起那个 (incomplete, "that what?")
RIGHT: 对不起那件事 (complete: "that matter/thing")
WRONG: 对不起打扰 (incomplete action)
RIGHT: 对不起打扰了 (completed action with 了)
PORTUGUESE:
WRONG: obrigado (when speaker is female)
RIGHT: obrigada (gender must match speaker in most contexts)
NOTE: In vocabulary lists, use "obrigado" as neutral unless context specifies
WRONG: literal word order from Spanish
RIGHT: natural Portuguese word order (may differ from Spanish)
